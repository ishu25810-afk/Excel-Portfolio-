* **Total Performance:** The business generated **$46,374** in total revenue with a healthy profit margin of **$16,231**.

* **Volume:** Successfully tracked the sale of **14,117 individual items** over a 3-year period (2021-2023).
* **Efficiency:** Analyzed daily transaction counts to identify "Peak Days" for better staff scheduling.
